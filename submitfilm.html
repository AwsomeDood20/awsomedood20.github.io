How to submit films

Step 1. Put your film in a .zip folder, if its a .vmm file, use Jayrods Vmm Cleaner.

Step 2. Upload the zipped file to MediaFire

Step 3. Copy the link after the upload.

Step 4. Send your film to ltfilms2022@gmail.com with a 394x442 pixel thumbnail.

Step 5. If you see your film in community films, CONGRATS! You now have a film on this website!
